Assistance from a Distance

We at CoAstra, aim to provide online assistance,guidance and suggestions, to all those who have been tested positive for Covid-19.
We strive to ensure your speedy recovery along with protecting your mental well-being.

Sign Up for our website, avail free online assistance and get reminder E-mails for the same.


Based On Your Condition Please Select The Level Of Assistance Required

MILD

  • Fever (< 100.4)
  • Sore Throat
  • Dry Cough
  • Chills
  • Headache
  • Body Aches
  • Loss of taste and smell
  • Tiredness

MODERATE

  • Difficulty in breathing
  • Troublesome Cough
  • High fever (> 100.4)
  • Severe Headache
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Diarrhoea
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Rashes on skin

SEVERE

  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain
  • Severe Cough
  • High fever (> 100.4)
  • Watery eyes / Swollen eyelids
  • Loss of speech
  • Bluish face / lips
  • Confusion or Unresponsiveness
