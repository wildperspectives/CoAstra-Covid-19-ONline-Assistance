Things To Do For Moderate Symptoms

Most infected people will develop moderate illness and recover without hospitalization and here's how one can overcome moderate symptoms:

  • Isolate yourself.
  • Frequent monitoring of Oxygen Level.
  • Inhale steam.
  • Consume lots of fluids.
  • Use lubricating eye drops.
  • Apply lotions and moisturizers.
  • Do breathing excercises at regular intervals.
  • Herbal home remedies including consumption of turmeric and ginger is recommended.
  • Use sanitizers and disinfectants.

Practice These Exercises To Protect Yourself

Surya Namaskar

It improves the blood circulation of all the important organs of the body, improves the functioning of the heart and lungs, strengthens the muscles of the arms and waist and makes the spine & waist more flexible.

Pawan Muktasana

Lie down in a straight position on your back or in a supine position, inhale and raise your legs at 90 degree. Exhale, bend your legs, and try to bring your knees towards your chest, clasp your knees by interlocking your fingers and raise your head and made touch your forehead with your knees.

Pranayama

First inhale with left nostril and exhale with right one, then inhale with right nostril and exhale with left nostril. Later continue the same process that is inhaling & exhaling with left and right nostrils alternately. Further switch to fast breathing by increasing the speed of breathing.
